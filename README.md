## About

I am a researcher working at the intersection of **human genetics, bioinformatics, and transcriptomic data analysis**, with a strong interest in understanding the molecular mechanisms underlying complex diseases. My work focuses on integrating computational analysis with biological insights to identify **disease-associated genes, immune pathways, and potential molecular biomarkers**.

My primary research interests involve the analysis of **RNA-Seq and transcriptomic datasets**, interpretation of large-scale genomic data, and investigation of **immunogenetic associations in autoimmune and inflammatory diseases**. I work with both public genomic resources and experimental datasets to explore gene expression patterns, pathway perturbations, and genetic susceptibility factors.

Through computational biology approaches, I aim to contribute to the advancement of **precision medicine, biomarker discovery, and translational genomics**.
